Jake's most delicious sandwich

Descreption

Jake's most delicious sandwich is, according to Jake, "the greatest sandwich [he's] ever made."

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Cream cheese
  • Pickles (from Prismo)
  • Dill
  • Eggs
  • Bird (from the window)
  • Cucumbers
  • Roma tomatoes
  • Sweet yellow onions
  • Tears (for salt)
  • Meat
  • Bacon
  • Lobster

Steps

  1. Sous-vide the meat with rosemary and thyme (135 degrees)
  2. Toast the baguette
  3. Smear cream cheese on both sides
  4. Put pickles on the bottom side and dill on the upper
  5. Hard boil the eggs and dice them, put on the pickles
  6. Prepare the bird, toss it on the sandwich
  7. Next, put diced cucumbers, tomatoes and onion on top
  8. Cry on the sandiwch a little
  9. Meat should be ready by now, toss it on the sandwich
  10. Fry some bacon, put it on top
  11. Now, for the most important part, put the lobster into boiling water and then wave at his sould to get into the sandwich
  12. Put both sides together and enjoy your perfect sandwich!
