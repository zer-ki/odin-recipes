Chilli con Carne

Descreption

A Spicy stew containing chili peppers (sometimes in the form of chili powder), meat (usually beef), tomatoes, and often pinto beans or kidney beans. Other seasonings may include garlic, onions, and cumin. The dish originated in northern Mexico or southern Texas.

Ingredients

  • Minced meat (preferably beef)
  • Tomato passata
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Red beans
  • Beef stock
  • Cumin powder
  • Smoked paprika
  • Chilli pepper
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Hot souce (optionally)

Steps

  1. Fry the meat on a pan with some oil
  2. Meanwhile, chop the onion and garlic
  3. Heat some olive oil in a pot, toss the onions in there
  4. Add garlic and fried meat
  5. Pour in the tomato passata and beef stock
  6. Add the spices and beans
  7. Let it cook for a while
  8. Done
